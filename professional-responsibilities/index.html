Language Arts Teacher

Responsibilities include:
  • Delivering and teaching Language Arts curriculum supported by multi-sensory/multi-media teaching methods.
  • Assessing student progress and utilizing assessments to plan future lessons accordingly.
  • Planning lessons in conjunction with IEP goals/benchmarks/objectives.
  • Collaborating and working with other department members to improve quality of instruction and materials.
  • Working closely with the Department Head to ensure quality class instruction.
  • Writing individualized quarterly reports for each student.
  • Maintaining contact with Academic Case Managers to maximize efficacy of instruction of all students.
  • Attending and participating in annual IEP meetings and 3 year reevaluations.

Language Arts Tutorial Teacher

Responsibilities include:
  • Delivering and teaching remedial language arts skills in one-on-one setting.
  • Assessing student progress and utilizing assessments to plan future lessons accordingly.
  • Incorporating feedback from Academic Case Manager to ensure quality of instruction.
  • Writing individualized quarterly reports for each student.
  • Attending and participating in annual IEP meetings and 3 year reevaluations.

Residential and Student Life Member

Responsibilities include:
  • Ensuring the safety of all students
  • Working in conjunction with Residential Coordinator and Houseparents
  • Communicating expectations to students and other staff
  • Initiating, coordinating and supervising on and off-campus activities
  • Modeling for students and other staff
