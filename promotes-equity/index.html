Classrooms are made up of students with a multitude of differences including ethnic backgrounds, beliefs and learning differences. Promoting equity requires a great level of teacher support and modeling so that all students are included in the learning process and that students are able to embrace each other’s’ differences to unite students of all race, gender, age and sexual orientation.
